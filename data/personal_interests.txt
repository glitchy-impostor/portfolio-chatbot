PERSONAL INTERESTS AND HOBBIES

SPORTS FANDOM

Football (Soccer):
Krish is a passionate FC Barcelona fan and supporter. He has followed Barcelona through various eras and considers himself a "culer" (the term for Barcelona fans). His love for Barcelona extends to a deep appreciation for the club's philosophy of "Més que un club" (More than a club).

Krish is an enormous fan of Lionel Messi, considering him the greatest player of all time. He has followed Messi's career closely, from his time at Barcelona through his subsequent moves. Watching Messi play is described by Krish as "watching someone operate in a different dimension of physics and possibility."

Memorable matches for Krish include the famous 6-2 El Clásico victory against Real Madrid, the 3-1 Champion's League Final win over Juventus and the 2022 World Cup Final

American Football:
Krish appreciates American football for its strategic complexity. He enjoys analyzing plays and understanding the tactical elements of the game.

Cricket:
Krish is a cricket fan who appreciates the sport's unique characteristics - including the fact that Test matches can last five days and still end in a draw. He enjoys both Test cricket and T20 formats.

Sports Analytics:
Beyond watching sports, Krish is deeply interested in sports analytics. He believes "sports analytics is just applied curiosity" and enjoys breaking down why plays work and what could have gone differently. He appreciates that "the numbers tell stories too - different stories than the highlights, but stories nonetheless."

ENTERTAINMENT AND MEDIA

How I Met Your Mother (HIMYM):
Krish is a fan of the TV show How I Met Your Mother. He appreciates the show's themes about friendship, the importance of having "a booth" or gathering place with friends, and the use of callbacks in storytelling. He believes that "every group of friends needs a booth - a place that becomes theirs through sheer repetition and the accumulation of inside jokes."

Notable HIMYM-related opinions:
- He strongly believes the HIMYM finale was bad (though he's open to hearing arguments defending it)
- He appreciates the "yellow umbrella" symbolism despite finding it "too on-the-nose"
- He values callbacks as "the highest form of humor"

Uncharted Video Games:
Krish is a fan of the Uncharted video game series featuring Nathan Drake. He connects with the series' themes of adventure and pursuit. The Drake family motto "Sic parvis magna" (Greatness from small beginnings) resonates with him.

He appreciates:
- The banter and humor in the games, especially Sully's one-liners
- Nathan Drake's journal-keeping habit
- The theme that "the treasure is never really the point - the pursuit is"
- The importance of being funny and human even in difficult situations

DEBATE

Krish has been an avid debater for a major portion of his life. He has been debating since being in 7th grade, and has excelled at it, Representing India at Harvard World Schools Debate in 2022 and Prague Debate Spring in 2022, representing his school Nationally at the Clifford Hicks Memorial Debate Finals in 2023, and also representing USC at USC IV, Princeton IV, UCLA IV, and Berkeley IV, finishing as Novice Finalist at Point Loma in 2024. He loves dissecting motions and understanding and learning from so many different perspectives you interact with at debate competitions.

PERSONAL PHILOSOPHY

Krish values curiosity and the intersection of his various interests. His approach to projects often connects things he loves - hence his interest in sports analytics combining technical skills with sports passion.

He appreciates storytelling, callbacks, and the idea that experiences and memories build on each other. He's drawn to the concept that "the best projects are the ones that teach you something you didn't know you needed to learn."
